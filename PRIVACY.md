# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in **Meds & Refills Tracker** ("the App").

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**Data Storage:** 
The App stores your medication tracking data, settings, and schedules locally on your device (and securely in your personal Apple iCloud account if enabled). We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal health information, medication data, or usage habits on our own servers. 

**Personal Information:** 
We do not require you to create an account with us, nor do we ask for your name, phone number, or other personally identifiable information.

**Third-Party Analytics and Tracking:** 
The App does not use third-party analytics or ad-tracking frameworks. Your data remains strictly private.

### 2. Notifications

The App requires access to iOS Push Notifications to send you medication and refill reminders. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use remote push notification servers to trigger these alerts.

### 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or Apple's App Store guidelines. Any updates will be posted on this page.
