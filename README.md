## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. How do I add a new medication?
Tap the "+" button on the main dashboard, enter the medication name, dosage, frequency, and time. Save the entry to start receiving reminders.

### 2. I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?
Ensure that you have granted the app permission to send push notifications. 
Go to **iOS Settings > Meds & Refills Tracker > Notifications** and toggle **Allow Notifications** to ON. Also, verify that your device is not in "Do Not Disturb" mode.

### 3. How do I track a refill?
When adding or editing a medication, fill in the "Current Quantity" and "Refill Threshold" fields. The app will automatically alert you when your supply drops below the threshold you set.

### 4. How do I edit or delete an existing medication?
Swipe left on any medication in your list to reveal the "Edit" and "Delete" options, or tap the medication to view its details and select edit.
